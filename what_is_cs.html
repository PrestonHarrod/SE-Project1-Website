What is Computer Science?

Computer scicence is simply put the study of computers and their computational systems. It involves a wide variety of tools such as programming, math, and analysis to develop software, improve system security, and create application features, just to name a few. For example, some languages you will learn and C++, C, Java, HTML, CSS and others. You will use other things such as MySQL, apache servers, Github and AWS.


Areas of study within computer science can include artifical intelligence, computer systems and networks, security, databases, graphic design, software engineering, and many more. Here are some of the classes you will be taking as a computer science major at Oklahoma Christian:

  • Software Engineering
  • Database Systems
  • Human Computer Interaction
  • Artificial Intelligence
