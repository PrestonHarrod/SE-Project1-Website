About Us: Jared Torp

Oklahoma Christian University

Computer Science With Cyber security

Indianapolis, Indiana

Favorite Food: Cajun

Hello! My name is Jared Torp and I am a computer science major with a focus in cyber security. I started my CS learning career about 4 years ago at Oklahoma Christian University. I am a senior graduating in the 2022 Spring semester and I hope to be working for the United States Air Force.

I first fell in love with technology at a young age. My father had a family computer in the basement of our Cincinnati home in 2002. In my free time as a kid, I would play computer games. The first computer games that I played were "Space Cadet Pinball" on the windows 95 Operating System, "Disney's Magic Artist Studio", and "VeggieTales: The Mystery of Veggie Island." There are a lot more computer games and video games I played as a kid, but those are the few that I remember.

I always had a love for computers and technology, so during the summer of 2019, I decided to attend Oklahoma Christian University to pursue a career in computer science! I found a love for problem solving and creating software during my years of college! I hope to use my knowledge for the United States Air Force and become a software engineer after graduation.
