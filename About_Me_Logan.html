About Us:

Logan Christopher

Hi! My name is Logan Christopher and I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am in my senior year at Oklahoma Christian University, and will graduate after the Fall 2022 semester. Upon graduating, I will begin working as a computer scientist for the Air Force at Tinker, AFB located in Oklahoma City, OK.

My CS journey started about 2 years ago while a friend of mine was showing me a few of his applications and programs. After looking over the source code and seeing how software is developed, I immediately began learning on my own. I started learning the basics of python and C++ before deciding to change my major to computer science. Once I finished my active duty service with the Coast Guard, I moved back to Oklahoma and transferred to Oklahoma Christian University.

The time I've spent at Oklahoma Christian University has been a rewarding one. My programming experience and knowledge has vastly improved since my self-learning days, and the support from the professors is a big part of my success thus far. I would recommend OC to anyone considering a career path in computer science.
