Hello! My name is Preston and I am a computer science major currently studying at Oklahoma Christian University.I plan to graduate in the spring of 2022 I plan to use my degree to be a software engineer or website developer in the OKC area

Ever since I was a kid I loved computers. I would always help my parents around the house fixing all the different things my parents coudln't with their technology. I was always look at the new technology coming out and wanting to get all the latest stuff. I got a Nintendo Gameboy SP when I was 7 and I've always had a spot for gaming ever since, even building my own computer in high school, where I learned about coding and found how rewarding and interesting it was.

In 2018, I came to Oklahoma Christain all the way from Washington because I had heard about what a great school it was, both for my faith and for the great computer science program they had. I'm so glad I decided to come here because I have learned so much about the computer science industry, made so many friends, and just all around had a ton of fun. The professors are great and knowledgeable and I would recommend anyone who wants their degree from a Christian perspective, Oklahoma Christian University is a excellent option.
