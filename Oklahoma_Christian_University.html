Oklahoma Christian University

Computer Science Program

  • Computer Science

    • This is a great degree plan for students who are interested in a career in research, computer science, a scientific focused company or an advanced degree in graduate school.

  • Computer Science with Business

    • This path is for individuals who are especially interested in a career solving problems with technology in business. This degree also provides a good Computer Science foundation so that someone with this degree would be qualified for any Computer Science Job.

  • Computer Science with Cybersecurity

    • Students who are especially interested in a career where they can use their cybersecurity skills to protect data and improve network security, this is the major for them. That includes practically every industry from finance to social media, government to healthcare and everything in between. Students with this degree would also be qualified for any Computer Science Job.

  • Computer Science with Gaming and Animation

    • This major is perfect if you are interested in a career with a company that uses 3D animation like healthcare, education, architecture, life sciences and of course, gaming and animation. This degree also provides a good Computer Science foundation so that someone with this degree would be qualified for any Computer Science Job.

  • Computer Science Dual Undergraduate & Graduate Degree

    • This option gives the student the opportunity to graduate with both levels of degree by taking some courses that double up in both the undergrad and graduate programs, and then completing remaining courses outlined for the graduate degree. This saves time and money, and the student can choose between four degree plans:

    • Artifical Intelligence
    • Cybersecurity
    • Data Science
    • Software Engineering
